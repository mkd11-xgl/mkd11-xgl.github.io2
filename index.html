What We Do

公司位于涂料之乡美誉的广东顺德，拥有工业漆技术研发、生产、质控、销售,技术服务队伍。产品质量稳定，服务周到，检测设备先进。主要生产智能锁漆和仿古艺术铜门漆及五金表面涂装的生产企业。调色匠遵循诚信经营，稳健发展，品质稳定的经营理念，得到上下游客户广泛认可和信赖，并致力成为五金涂料喷涂行业的优秀供应商。

Located in Shunde, Guangdong, renowned as the "Hometown of Coatings", we possess a complete team for industrial paint technology R&D, production, quality control, sales, and technical services. With stable product quality, attentive service, and advanced testing equipment, we mainly produce smart lock paint, antique art door paint, and hardware surface coatings. Adhering to the business philosophy of integrity, steady development, and consistent quality, Tiaosejiang has gained wide recognition from clients throughout the supply chain, and is committed to becoming an excellent supplier in the hardware coating industry.
