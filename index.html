What We Do

公司位于涂料之乡美誉的广东顺德，拥有工业漆技术研发、生产、质控、销售,技术服务队伍。产品质量稳定，服务周到，检测设备先进。 主要生产智能锁漆和仿古艺术铜门漆及五金表面涂装的生产企业。 调色匠遵循诚信经营，稳健发展，品质稳定的经营理念，得到上下游客户广泛认可和信赖，并致力成为五金涂料喷涂行业的优秀供应商。

The company is located in Shunde, Guangdong, which is known as the hometown of paint. It has industrial paint technology research and development, production, quality control, sales, and technical service teams. The product quality is stable, the service is thoughtful, and the testing equipment is advanced. It is a manufacturer mainly producing smart locks, antique art copper doors and hardware surface coating. Colorist adheres to the business philosophy of honest management, steady development, and stable quality, and has been widely recognized and trusted by upstream and downstream customers, and is committed to becoming an excellent supplier in the hardware coating industry.
